The **Segway Xafari Electric Bike** is designed for adventurous riders who want comfort, range, and smart features in a rugged all-terrain package. Built with a powerful 750W motor and equipped with advanced suspension, fat tires, and long-range battery tech, the Xafari is made to handle everything from daily commutes to dirt trails.

This intelligent eBike includes Segway’s smart features like **Apple Find My**, **GPS**, **AirLock anti-theft tech**, and **Segway-Ninebot App** integration. Whether you’re tackling rough roads or cruising in the city, the **Xafari** delivers confident control, premium performance, and a connected ride.
