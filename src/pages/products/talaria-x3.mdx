The **Talaria X3** is an electric bike designed for **adventure seekers and thrill riders**, with a focus on **power, performance, and durability**. Whether you're riding on rugged trails or exploring new off-road terrains, the Talaria X3 Concept offers an exhilarating experience.

Equipped with a **4.5 kW peak power** motor, the **Talaria X3** delivers a top speed of **32 mph** and a range of up to **70 km** on a single charge. It comes with two battery options: a **20Ah lithium-ion battery** for those who need a lighter option and a **40Ah lithium-ion battery** for maximum range and power.

The high-performance **hydraulic fork** and **adjustable rear suspension** ensure a smooth ride across various terrains, while safety is prioritized with **regenerative braking** and robust **disc brakes**. The user-friendly **LCD display** keeps you informed about your ride status.
