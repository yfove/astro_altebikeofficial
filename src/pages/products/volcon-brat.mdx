The **Volcon Brat** is a rugged and versatile electric motorcycle designed for both **urban commuting** and **off-road adventures**. Whether you’re cruising through the city or tackling rough trails, the **Volcon Brat** offers powerful performance and top-tier comfort with cutting-edge features.

Powered by a **750W motor**, the **Volcon Brat** reaches speeds of up to **28+ mph** and boasts an impressive **70+ mile range** in its most efficient mode. This electric motorcycle is built for long rides, quick city commutes, and tackling various terrains, all while offering exceptional reliability and fun.

With its **inverted telescoping adjustable front forks** and **monoshock rear suspension**, the Brat is designed to deliver a smooth ride, whether you're navigating bumpy trails or smooth roads. The **hydraulic disc brakes** ensure superior stopping power, making the **Volcon Brat** as safe as it is thrilling.

**Volcon Brat** is equipped with a **4” LED color display**, Bluetooth connectivity, and USB charging port in the lockable storage for added convenience. The LED headlights and tail/brake lights ensure excellent visibility during your rides.
